Target Customer Profile

The Independent Garage

Independent garages and workshops running 1–10 bays, managing MOTs, services, and repairs. Operating with paper job cards, spreadsheets, or basic tools — ready for a proper garage management system.

MOT Testing General Repair Car Servicing Tyre Fitting Diagnostics

Key Pain Points to Listen For

  • Paper-based job management — Job cards get lost, no visibility of work in progress
  • No online booking — MOT and service bookings done manually by phone
  • Stock blindness — No idea what parts are on the shelf or when to reorder
  • Revenue leakage — Deferred work never followed up, jobs fall through the cracks
  • Manual quoting — Looking up parts and labour times manually, slow and error-prone
  • No customer follow-up — No automated reminders for MOTs, services, or deferred work

Buying Triggers to Watch For

  • Growing from 1–2 bays to 3+ and losing control of the diary
  • Hired a service advisor and need a proper booking system
  • Lost customers because of missed MOT reminders
  • Expanding into MOT testing and need compliant diary management
  • Want to offer online booking to compete with franchised dealers
